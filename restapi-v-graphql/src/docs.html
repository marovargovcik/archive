Schema:

Entity: Office [offices, office(officeCode)]

  • employees: Array of Employee

Entity: Employee [employees, employee(employeeNumber)]

  • office: Office
  • supervisor: Employee
  • supervisees: Array of Employee
  • customers: Array of Customer

Entity: Customer [customers, customer(customerNumber)]

  • salesRepresentative: Employee
  • orders: Array of Order
  • payments: Array of Payment

Entity: Order [orders, order(orderNumber)]

  • customer: Customer
  • orderDetails: Array of OrderDetail

Entity: Order details [can only be queried as part of order]

  • product: Product

Entity: ProductLine [productLines, productLine(productLine)]

  • products: Array of Product

Entity: Product [products, product(productCode)]

  • productLine: ProductLine
  • orders: Array of Order

Entity: Payments [payments, payment(checkNumber)]

  • customer: Customer
